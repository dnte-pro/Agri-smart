# AgriSmart – MVP Product Description

## Overview

AgriSmart is a smart farm management platform designed to help farmers plan, monitor, and manage crop and livestock production efficiently. The platform provides a centralized digital system for organizing farm activities, receiving timely recommendations, tracking production, and improving decision-making throughout the farming cycle.

The MVP focuses on five core modules:

## 1. Dashboard

The Dashboard provides a real-time overview of farm operations, including:

* Active crops and livestock.
* Upcoming and overdue farm tasks.
* Weather updates and farming recommendations.
* Farm productivity summaries.
* Disease and pest alerts relevant to the farmer's selected crops.

The Dashboard serves as the farmer's command center for daily farm management.

---

## 2. Crop Management

The crop management module is powered by a structured agricultural knowledge base rather than manually entered crop information.

When a farmer selects a crop, such as cabbage, the system automatically loads:

* All available cabbage varieties.
* Recommended growing conditions.
* Growth stages and maturity timelines.
* Fertilizer and nutrient recommendations.
* Common pests affecting cabbage.
* Common cabbage diseases and symptoms.
* Prevention and treatment recommendations.
* Irrigation requirements.
* Harvesting guidelines.

This same structure applies to all crops available in the platform. Each crop has its own dedicated profile containing:

* Crop varieties.
* Recommended farming practices.
* Pest information.
* Disease information.
* Treatment recommendations.
* Yield expectations.

### Automated Crop Scheduling

After planting, the system automatically generates a crop lifecycle schedule based on the selected crop variety and planting date.

Scheduled activities may include:

* Land preparation.
* Planting.
* Irrigation.
* Fertilizer application.
* Weeding.
* Pest inspections.
* Disease monitoring.
* Harvesting.
* The farmer can also add aditional activities in the farm
* Soil testing - The farmer can take sample soils to best 

Tasks are displayed on the Dashboard and farmers can mark them as completed.

### Yield Tracking

Farmers can record:

* Harvest quantities.
* Harvest dates.
* Yield performance.

This information helps farmers evaluate productivity over time.

---

## 3. Livestock Management

The livestock module supports management of:

* Cattle
* Goats
* Sheep
* Poultry
* Pigs

Farmers can record:

* Animal inventory.
* Feeding schedules.
* Vaccination schedules.
* Health records.
* Treatments administered.
* Production outputs such as milk, eggs, or weight gain.
* Reproduction schedule

This enables better livestock planning and record keeping.

---

## 4. Weather and Advisory System

The platform integrates weather forecasting services to provide:

* Current weather conditions.
* Multi-day forecasts.
* Rainfall predictions.
* Temperature trends.

Based on weather conditions and selected crops, the system generates contextual recommendations such as:

* Irrigation reminders.
* Disease-risk alerts.
* Fertilizer application recommendations.
* Harvest planning suggestions.
* Pest monitoring warnings.

The weather API is also culminated to the application to give daily weather and also predict the weather for the coming days
This helps farmers make timely decisions and reduce avoidable losses.

---

## 5. Agricultural Knowledge Base Administration

The platform includes an administration module used by agricultural experts, agronomists, extension officers, or system administrators.

This module serves as the central source of agricultural intelligence that powers recommendations across the platform.

Administrators can manage:

### Crop Data

* Crop categories.
* Crop varieties.
* Growth stages.
* Planting requirements.
* Harvesting guidelines.

### Pest Management Data

* Pest profiles.
* Pest symptoms.
* Prevention methods.
* Treatment recommendations.

### Disease Management Data

* Disease profiles.
* Disease symptoms.
* Causes.
* Prevention measures.
* Treatment recommendations.

### Fertilizer and Nutrient Data

* Fertilizer types.
* Application schedules.
* Crop-specific recommendations.

### Livestock Data

* Animal breeds.
* Vaccination schedules.
* Common diseases.
* Treatment recommendations.
* Feeding guidelines.
* Gestation Schedule and periods

Changes made within the knowledge base are immediately reflected in recommendations and schedules generated for farmers.

---

## User Roles

### Farmer

Can:

* Manage crops.
* Manage livestock.
* View weather forecasts.
* Receive recommendations.
* Track farm activities.
* Record harvests and production.

### Administrator / Agricultural Expert

Can:

* Manage agricultural knowledge.
* Add and update crop varieties.
* Manage pest and disease information.
* Update livestock health records and schedules.
* Maintain farming recommendations used across the platform.

---

## MVP Goal

The goal of the MVP is to provide farmers with a digital farming assistant that combines:

* Crop management.
* Livestock management.
* Automated farm scheduling.
* Weather intelligence.
* Agricultural recommendations based on yields.
* Production tracking.
* Expert-managed agricultural knowledge.

By bringing these capabilities into a single platform, Shamba Mkakati helps farmers improve planning, increase productivity, reduce losses, and make better farming decisions regardless of their location.
